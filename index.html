Key Features

      

        

          
Illusion-of-Security™

          

            Our flagship framework. Packed with buzzwords, diagrams, and just enough
            pseudo-math to look legitimate in screenshots. The perfect layer to hide
            behind when someone asks, “So how does it actually work?”
          

        

        

          
Zero-Knowledge Fog Compression

          

            Inspired by ZK proofs and fog machines at techno clubs. We compress information
            into a dense cloud of “don’t worry about it,” ideal for CT threads and panel talks.
          

        

        

          
Multi-Layer Opacity Consensus

          

            A consensus model where nobody is fully sure what’s happening, but it looks
            amazing in a whitepaper. The more layers we add, the more opaque it becomes —
            just like most tokenomics.
          

        

        

          
Pro Dev Roleplay Culture

          

            PVC is built for memers, “devs” and serious builders who enjoy the joke
            but still want to ship something. You can act like a pro dev, surrounded
            by others doing exactly the same.
          

        

      

    


    
    

      
Tokenomics

      

        PVC is designed as a fair-launch style token on Solana, with almost the entire supply
        flowing directly to the market and community. No hidden allocations, no surprise unlocks.
        The structure is simple and transparent so holders clearly understand who owns what.

Philosophy

          

            The goal is to keep the tokenomics as honest and readable as possible:
            most of the supply is in the hands of the community and the open market.
            Any locked or reserved allocations (dev, marketing, listings) are kept small,
            publicly communicated, and trackable on-chain.
          

        

      


      

        Note: Exact numbers can be updated to match the final on-chain configuration,
        but the principle remains the same: community-first, fair-style distribution.
